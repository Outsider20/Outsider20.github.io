Rithmomachia
Game Board
The Rithmomachia game board is a grid-based battlefield where players strategically maneuver their pieces to capture their opponent's pieces.
Piece Movement
Each piece in Rithmomachia has a unique numerical value, and the movement and capturing of pieces are based on complex mathematical relationships between these values.
Game Mechanics
Rithmomachia is a deep and challenging game that requires players to think strategically about their moves, anticipate their opponent's actions, and leverage the mathematical properties of the pieces.
